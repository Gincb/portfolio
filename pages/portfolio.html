Basic Web

Main goal

Create a responsive website, that adapts to different viewports, mainly mobile, tablet and desktop by applying „mobile first“ principle, starting off from a small scale viewport and moving on to larger scale.

The design of the website was influenced by a certain style or movement, in this case – Swiss Style, by conducting research to define the style and taking in regard used images, typography, inherited or inspired aspects of the style, characteristics and definitions.

Achievements

Implementation of three key components to create a responsive design: use of advanced css structures with grid, by applying „column drop“ layout – from a multi-column layout for desktop devices to dropping columns along the way to one for mobile, use of flexible images to adapt to different viewports and media queries.

Creation of a strong and consistent visuals by utilizing the Gestalt Principles and conducting usability testing „Trunk Test“ to find flaws in the UI and improve them.

Learning the innerworkings of Adobe XD, utilizing the functionalities and tools provided to create a high-fidelity prototype.

Basic Content

Main goal

Building a campaign website for a customer, prompting users to participate in volunteering or raising awareness of food waste. Film and edit a video reflecting the campaigns primary goal and idea, engaging users to participate in Foodsharing and stop food waste.

Achievements

By assigning individual tasks and working together in a group creating a campaign website for a company, getting a direct experience trying to fulfill customers wishes and following their design guidelines.

Sketching and planning the campaign video by creating a storyboard, summarizing each scene and getting to know more about compositions, shot types, angles, audio etc. to get the best out of every set, planning and executing. Our video focuses on a more storytelling, main character is the cabbage, who imagines itself being in a relationship with a man, who bought it. By the end of the story, the man leaves the cabbage untouched for too long, it gets spoiled and ends up in the trash. That’s why our main campaign slogan is #LittleHeartbreaks.

In customers pitch, we focus on delivering our campaign message and concept for the client, explaining the meaning of the video, different website design decisions followed by guidelines. Explain how we deal with food waste ourselves and trying to raise awareness by giving an example of „Compound Effect“ – taking care of our grocories and food for 15 minutes everyday – would that make an impact?
